back/lay ?
example match :- IND vs AUS
match odds/rating :
 __________________________
|IND | 1.80 back | 1.82 lay|
|AUS | 2.24 back | 2.28 lay|

back example :
person A : bet 20k back @india (he wants to win india)
	if IND wins 16k profit
	if AUS wins 20k loss

lay example:
person B : bet 20k lay @india(he wants to win Australia) 
	if IND wins 16440 loss
	if AUS wins 20k profit
